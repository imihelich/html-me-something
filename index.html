Good Dogs & Dog-Adjacent Demons

A collection of the most memorable pups from my time working at a dog kennel/daycare.

Kevin A.K.A. The King of Hell

Kevin is horrible. He's bratty and thinks that he's top dog, even though he's the size of a football. It's impossible to hate him though... somehow.

Sheldon A.K.A. Mama's Boy

The owner's dachshund. A handsome little weenie with a bowtie. Demands to be held.

Riley A.K.A. Sweet & Spicy

Loves hugs and naps. Warning: Interrupting naptime may prove hazardous to your health.

Nico & Sofie A.K.A. The Dynamic Duo

The italian greyhound siblings. No conclusive evidence that they aren't physically attached to one another. Might actually be one two-headed dog?

Rex A.K.A. The Hot Mess

Rex is an instagram thottie trapped in a dog's body. He demands all your attention & contorts into questionable positions for the perfect pic.

April A.K.A. Cuddle Puddle

April is the perfect sweetheart. She's always mindful of where she's putting her paws when jumping up for hugs so that she doesn't smack her human in the face.
